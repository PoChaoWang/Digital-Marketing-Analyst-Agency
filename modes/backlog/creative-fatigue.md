# Creative Fatigue Analysis Mode

## 使用情境

使用者詢問 creative fatigue、素材疲乏、CTR 下降、frequency 過高、Meta creative 表現、ad format 表現時使用此 mode。

## 診斷框架

Creative fatigue 通常需要時間序列或比較期。檢查：

- frequency rising
- CTR or outbound CTR declining
- CPC or CPM rising
- CPA rising or CVR declining
- spend concentration by creative/ad
- audience saturation
- creative format differences such as `car`, `img`, `col`, `vid`

## 必查資料

- ad / creative / ad format
- impressions, reach, frequency if available
- clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM
- conversions, CPA, ROAS
- daily trend or comparison period
- placement and audience if available

## 輸出要求

- Fatigue risk by creative/ad/ad format
- Evidence and confidence
- What to verify next
- Creative testing recommendation
- Risk

若缺少 frequency 或 trend data，只能標記 `fatigue risk cannot be confirmed`，不可直接斷言素材疲乏。

遇到 format code 時，先讀 `docs/GLOSSARY.md`。
